# Renewal Risk Playbook

## Situation
The customer's health score has dropped below 50, usage has declined significantly over the last 30 days, or the renewal date is approaching within 90 days with unresolved concerns.

## Guidance
Renewal risk is the highest-priority decision point. Act immediately when multiple signals converge — declining usage, open support tickets, and approaching renewal dates create a compounding risk. The goal is to re-engage the customer before they begin evaluating alternatives.

## Recommended Actions
1. **Schedule an urgent executive alignment call** within 48 hours to understand root concerns directly from the customer's decision-maker.
2. **Prepare a value realization report** showing concrete ROI metrics the customer has achieved since onboarding, highlighting features they may be underutilizing.
3. **Resolve all open support tickets** before the alignment call — unresolved issues during renewal discussions signal neglect.
4. **Offer a tailored success plan** with clear milestones for the next 30/60/90 days that addresses their specific pain points.
5. **Engage your executive sponsor internally** to prepare for potential pricing or contract flexibility discussions if needed.
