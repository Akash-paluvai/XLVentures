# Healthy Account Playbook

## Situation
The customer's health score is above 80, usage is stable or growing, there are no open escalations, and the relationship with the champion is strong. The renewal is not imminent or is expected to close smoothly.

## Guidance
Healthy accounts are the foundation of net revenue retention, but they are also the most neglected. The risk with healthy accounts is complacency — if you stop proactively delivering value, the customer's perception shifts from "strategic partner" to "utility vendor," making them vulnerable to competitors or budget cuts. Use this stable period to deepen the relationship and plant seeds for expansion.

## Recommended Actions
1. **Maintain a regular cadence of check-ins** (monthly or bi-monthly) even when there is nothing urgent — consistency builds trust.
2. **Share proactive insights** such as benchmarking data, industry trends, or new feature announcements that are relevant to their use case.
3. **Identify internal advocates** beyond the primary champion — build relationships with 2–3 additional stakeholders to reduce single-point-of-failure risk.
4. **Document success stories** from this account that can be used for case studies, references, or internal training — this also reinforces the customer's own perception of value.
5. **Explore lightweight expansion opportunities** such as new feature adoption, additional integrations, or training sessions that increase stickiness without requiring a formal upsell motion.
